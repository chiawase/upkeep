It's okay.

We know how hard it is to constantly keep track of everything.


Upkeep is a mobile application specifically designed for beginning businessmen who are in the nano, micro and small enterprises. With Upkeep, keeping track of inventory and financials do not need to be as hectic as it usually it is when done on the traditional ledgers. Upkeep is a cloud-based app that wil keep your business information secure and safe in your Upkeep account.
